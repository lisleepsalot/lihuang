Atlas Swing
As a submission to the 2023 Geopipe NYC game jam, Atlas Swings is a golf game crafted utilizing Geopipe's provided model of New York City. The game secured first place in the game jam. In my role as the artist and graphic designer on the team, I collaborated with my colleagues to conduct research and delve into art styles and game flow. More specifically, I was responsible for designing and creating 3D models, 2D UI elements, and particle effects for various assets.
