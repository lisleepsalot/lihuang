THE ANATOMY
OF FASCISM

This book project merges the writings of Robert O. Paxton's "The Anatomy of Fascism" and Adolf Loos' "Ornament and Crime" using both sans-serif and black-letter typefaces. Its purpose is to delve into the harsh realities of modernity and examine how they influence design and readability.
