"Hospital Navigation" represents the culmination of my work in Contingent Cartographies within the realm of digital and interactive experiences. This deeply personal project documents the overwhelming experience of accompanying my grandfather during his battle with illness at the end of 2022. Drawing upon my own subjective feelings, my intention is to recreate the stressful and bureaucratic hospital environment.
Through a series of personal narratives, I have sought to create a sense of navigation that allows the viewer to experience the space through my own perspective.
