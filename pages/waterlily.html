ATLAS SWINGS

As an entry in the 2023 Geopipe NYC game jam, Atlas Swings is a golf game built using the Geopipe-provided model of New York City. The game won first place in the game jam. As the artist and graphic designer of the team, I collaborated with my team to research and explore art styles and game flow.
